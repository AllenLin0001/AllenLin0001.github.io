Research interests: I am broadly interested in machine learning, data ming, and information retrieval, with a special emphasis on AI-powered user-centered systems. Specifically, my work so far focuses on leveraging deep reinforcement learning and natural language processing to enhance personalization and fairness in recommendation.

About me: I am currently a 2nd year PhD student advised by Prof. James Caverlee. Before that, I earned a Master's degree in Computer Science from Duke University and a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from The Ohio State University.
